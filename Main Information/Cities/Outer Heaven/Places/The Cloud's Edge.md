# Overview: 

A bustling, often rowdy tavern in [[Outer Heaven]], known for its potent ale and being a prime spot for rumors and quest-finding. It's a common haunt for new and mid-tier adventurers, as well as those looking to discreetly exchange information.

# Atmosphere:

- **Lively & Loud:** Constant chatter, clinking mugs, and occasional brawls.
- **Warm & Smoky:** Fireplace in the corner, scent of cheap ale and pipe smoke.
- **Diverse Crowd:** Mix of races and classes, from grizzled warriors to eager mages.

# Key Features:

- **"The Rumor Board":** A physical board near the entrance where patrons pin job requests, missing person notices, or cryptic messages.
- **Strong Ale:** Known for its cheap, potent brew that loosens tongues.
- **Private Booths:** A few secluded booths for more discreet conversations, though sound still carries.

# [[Kaelen 'Ironhand' Stonefist |Barkeep Ironhand]]
**Name:** [[Kaelen 'Ironhand' Stonefist]]

**Description:** Kaelen is a gruff, broad-shouldered barkeep with grey-streaked red hair and a face etched with old scars, hinting at his legendary past. He runs [[The Cloud's Edge]] with a watchful eye, quick to quell trouble but a fount of hard-earned wisdom for earnest adventurers. He's a quiet observer, capable of offering cryptic advice or crucial information for those who earn his respect.
