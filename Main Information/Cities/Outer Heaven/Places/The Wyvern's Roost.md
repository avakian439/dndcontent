### Overview & Purpose:
An upscale inn and eatery in [[Outer Heaven]], known for its refined atmosphere, quality fare, and as a prime location for veteran adventurers and wealthy merchants to conduct discreet business. It's where serious contracts are forged and high-stakes information is exchanged.

#### Atmosphere & Ambiance:
- Describe the sensory experience of being in this location.
    - **Visuals:** Rich wood paneling, plush seating, soft magical lighting.
    - **Sounds:** Muted conversations, soft music, clinking of fine silverware.
    - **Scents:** Scent of exotic spices and aged spirits.
    - **General Feel:** Quiet, refined, elegant, and warm. It attracts an exclusive crowd of experienced, well-dressed individuals; less boisterous than other taverns.
#### Key Features & Layout:
- What are the most important or interesting aspects of this location?
    - **Private Dining Rooms**: Several sound-dampened rooms available for truly confidential meetings.
    - **Gourmet Menu**: Offers a selection of rare delicacies and fine wines/spirits, priced accordingly.
    - **Discreet Staff**: Waitstaff are professional and observant, but trained in discretion.
    - **Secure Lodgings**: High-quality rooms available for extended stays, often with additional security measures.
- General layout and important areas: Common areas, Private Dining Rooms, Lodgings.

#### History & Reputation:
- Brief origin story or significant past events related to the building.
    - **Origin**: Established fifty years ago by a collective of retired master spies, it was designed from the ground up to be a neutral, secure ground for delicate negotiations. More recently, it was shrewdly acquired and elevated to its current premier status by its proprietor, [[Lady Valerius Thorne]].
    - **Significant Event**: Rumored to have hosted the signing of the 'Silver Accord' – a critical peace treaty between two warring guilds that saved Outer Heaven from internal strife.
- How others generally perceive the location.
    - **Perception**: Universally respected as a safe, uncompromised venue for serious dealings. Its reputation for discretion is legendary, largely due to the meticulous oversight of [[Lady Valerius Thorne]], making it the preferred choice for those who value secrecy above all else.

#### Staff & Important NPCs:
The formidable [[Lady Valerius Thorne]] serves as the proprietor, overseeing every detail with shrewd precision. The staff at [[The Wyvern's Roost]] are professional, observant, and highly discreet, always impeccably dressed in tailored suits and ties. They are trained to provide impeccable service while maintaining the privacy of their high-profile clientele, ensuring that conversations remain confidential and interactions are seamless. They operate with unwavering loyalty to their demanding proprietor.
#### Current Situation & Hooks:
- What is currently happening at or around the location?
    - **Current Situation**: A prominent, but reclusive, artifact dealer, Lady Isolde, has taken up long-term residence in one of the secure lodgings, conducting all her business exclusively within the inn's private rooms.
- Potential quests, rumors, or secrets tied to the building.
    - **Quest Hook**: A desperate merchant seeks an introduction to Lady Isolde, believing she possesses a rare component vital to saving his failing business, but the staff are impenetrable.
    - **Rumor**: Whispers circulate about a new, highly valuable information broker operating out of the Wyvern's Roost, selling secrets that could destabilize several major factions.
